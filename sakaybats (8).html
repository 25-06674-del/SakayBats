About the Project

Why SakayBats?

Public transportation in Batangas City has long operated on unwritten, unstandardized routes. Newcomers, students from other provinces, and tourists frequently board the wrong jeepney, overpay for fares, or waste time searching for terminals.

Existing tools like Google Maps and word-of-mouth fail to capture the nuances of Batangas City's local transport network. SakayBats was developed to fill that gap — a community-validated, interactive, freely accessible guide for every commuter.

Project Objectives

1

Design and develop a functional website that displays interactive maps and point-to-point route information for public transportation in Batangas City.

2

Gather and validate accurate jeepney and tricycle route data through interviews with city administrators, drivers, dispatchers, and regular commuters.

3

Evaluate usability and effectiveness by testing SakayBats with students, workers, and tourists, measuring navigation improvement.
